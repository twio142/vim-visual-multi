<objective>
Close the two wiring gaps found by the v1.0 milestone audit:
1. `highlight.define_groups()` is never called from `M.setup()` — VM_ highlight groups are undefined at runtime, making all extmark cursor/selection rendering invisible to the user.
2. `g_increment` does not call `undojoin` between cursor iterations — N cursors produce N undo entries instead of 1, breaking the FEAT-06 undo grouping contract.

Both fixes are 1-2 line changes with high impact. Each requires a corresponding spec to prevent regression.

Purpose: Satisfy FEAT-07 (highlight groups defined at plugin load), FEAT-06 and FEAT-10 (g_increment undo grouping correct for multi-cursor).
Output: Patched init.lua and edit.lua, extended highlight_spec.lua and edit_spec.lua, 100% passing test suite.
</objective>

      -- Must be exactly 1 new undo entry for all 3 cursor operations combined
